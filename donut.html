Doughboys

Web Design and Illustration

For my final project in a web design class at the Corcoran College of Art and Design, I decided to make a website all about doughnuts.

The site will tell you about the history of doughnuts, different varieties and give you some recipes. There are also some descriptions of notable doughnut destinations and their specialties.
