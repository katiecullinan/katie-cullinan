Dinosaur in Your Pocket

UX Design + Prototyping

Challenge
Create an experiential movie trailer that builds anticipation, creates buzz, and sells tickets opening weekend.

Solution
We created a mobile app game that allows players to make their own dinosaur, raise it and train it for the world of Jurassic Park. Users will be able to interact with their own dinosaur, Jurassic Park merchandise and play with friends through an augmented reality interface. Players "transport" their dinosaur to Jurassic Park by purchasing a movie ticket.

VCU Brandcenter
Experience Design | Fall 2015
Team: Mason Brown + Dan Cotting
