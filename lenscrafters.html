LensCrafters: See Your Future

UX Design + Prototyping

Challenge
Encourage freshman and sophomore college students to embrace their newfound independence and take responsibility for their health by going to LensCrafters for an eye exam.

Solution
Using a psychic reading theme we created a campaign to lead students to a kiosk that they will look into to answer yes or no questions that assess their risk for certain eye problems. At the end of the test the student is awarded with branded sunglasses or a tarot card that will encourage them to visit their closest LensCrafters. Also, an Instagram campaign prompts students to post selfies to "see their future" by using their photos to show what they would look like in glasses.

Take Our Test

VCU Brandcenter
Prototyping 1 | Fall 2015
Team: Abby Walter + Stanley Hines
